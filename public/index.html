                                  The Platform

                                  DEW (Digital Eco Warden) is an IoT-powered plant care platform. ESP32 sensors stream moisture, temperature, and light to a single dashboard. Smart alerts and a gentle AI guardian help you act before plants need help—so you can grow with confidence.

                                  Why DEW?

                                  Plant care should be accessible and stress-free. We give you the right data at the right time: moisture, light, temperature, and actionable alerts. Whether you’re a beginner or a green thumb, every plant can thrive with DEW.

                                  Our Vision

                                  A world where technology and nature work together. DEW is the bridge between your plants and you—with a growing community where wardens share tips, favourite plants, and support each other in building greener spaces.

                                  How It Works

                                  Sensors in your pots send data to the cloud. The dashboard shows live readings, trends, and alerts. When moisture drops or conditions change, you get notified.

                                  DEW Platform

                                  Components and benefits of the Digital Eco Warden system.

                                  Hardware & data

                                  Components

                                  DEW is built around ESP32-compatible sensors that measure soil moisture, ambient temperature, and light levels. Each sensor streams readings to a central backend via HTTP or MQTT. The server stores telemetry, merges it with plant profiles, and serves a REST API used by the dashboard and mobile clients. The dashboard is a single-page app that shows live metrics, historical charts, and configurable alerts. User data—favourites, plant usage, profile, and alerts—is persisted in JSON and can be backed by a database. This vertical integration—sensors, API, and frontend—delivers a single pipeline from your pots to your screen.

                                  Impact

                                  Project benefits

                                  The platform gives you real-time visibility into plant health so you can water and adjust conditions before plants suffer. Threshold-based alerts (e.g. moisture below 40%) notify you via the app, reducing guesswork and overwatering. By tracking which plants you use in the dashboard and deskbot, DEW surfaces a meaningful “plants” count and encourages consistent care. Favourite plants and profile stats support a community layer where wardens share preferences and follow each other. Data stays under your control with optional cloud backup. The stack is designed to scale from a single pot to many zones and from hobby use to shared or educational deployments.
